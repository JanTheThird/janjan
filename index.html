HOW TO REACH MYTHIC IN MLBB

To reach Mythic in Mobile Legends, master meta heroes and counter-picks, focus on objective control like turrets and the Lord instead of just kills, and maintain map awareness to anticipate enemy movements. Adjust your hero role to fit the team composition, improve your farming efficiency by getting last hits on minions, and stay focused with a positive mental attitude (PMA).


PRE GAME & HERO SELECTION

  • Learn the Meta: Understand which heroes are currently powerful and learn to play at least a few from different roles.
  • Counter Pick: Select heroes that counter the enemy's composition to address potential problems and gain an advantage.
  • Be Versatile: Have mastery over various hero types (marksman, fighter, mage) and know how to play different roles, especially Tank or Support, to fill team needs.
  • Master Builds & Emblems: Develop your own builds based on your playstyle and current match, and ensure your Emblem sets are maxed out (level 60).

IN GAME STRATEGY

  • Map Awareness: Frequently check the mini-map to stay aware of enemy positions and potential threats.
  • Farming Efficiency: Maximize your gold and experience by securing last hits on minions and taking every opportunity to farm efficiently.
  • Strategic Pushing: Push lanes strategically, but also know when to group with your team to take objectives.
  • Objective Focus: Prioritize taking turrets and securing objectives like the Turtle and Lord, as these are crucial for victory, according to YouTube.
  • Lane Management: Use tactics like lane freezing to deny enemies experience and farm, then use minion waves to create a powerful push.
